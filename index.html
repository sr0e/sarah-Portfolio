Projects

The following projects were initially completed during my Data Science Bootcamp in 2024. They have been improved upon since graduating.

Automated Classification of Brain Tumor MRI Images with CNN Modeling

This project uses CNNs to classify brain tumors based on MRI images, they employs grad-CAM to overlay a headmap on the images. Finally, the best CNN model (Xception architecture) and grad-CAM was incorporated into a streamlit app.

TruthNet: Developing a Natural Language Model for Fake News Detection

The ability to recognize factual news from misinformation is crucial. The objective of this project is to develop a natural language processing model able to effectively predict whether a reddit post is from the subreddit of World News or from a satirical news subreddit, The Onion, based on its textual content.

Preventing Opioid Abuse with Machine Learning Classification Methods

This project aims to develop a machine learning model to accurately predict the likelihood of a patient abusing pain medications. By leveraging classification algorithms, such as Random Forest, Logistic Regression, and Neural Networks, the project seeks to provide actionable insights that enable healthcare professionals to make informed decisions regarding the prescription and management of pain medications.
